Grade Guide

Finding the Right Grade for Your Product

Culinary Grade

Robust flavor profile designed to hold up in baking, blended beverages, ice cream, and large-scale food production. High yield, consistent color.

Ideal for: Cafés, Food Manufacturing, RTD Beverages

Premium Grade

Balanced sweetness and umami. Suitable for lattes, premium confectionery, and mid-tier retail products. Smooth texture, vibrant green.

Ideal for: Specialty Cafés, Retail, Hospitality

Ceremonial Grade

Single-origin, first-harvest leaves. Drunk straight with water. Deep sweetness, minimal bitterness, complex aroma. Reserved for the highest-end applications.

Ideal for: Luxury Retail, Tea Ceremonies, Gifting
