Overview

Patience Becomes Flavor.

"Unlocking the true aroma sleeping within the leaves."

Standard PET bottled tea is brewed fast and hot. ARTERRA's cold-extraction process takes 30× longer — drawing every drop at low temperature to preserve the full spectrum of theanine, catechins, and volatile aromatics that heat destroys.

The result is a depth of sweetness unlike anything currently on the market. No added sugars. No artificial flavor enhancement. Just what the leaf gives when you give it time.

The Process

From Leaf to Bottle

01

Leaf Selection

Only select-grade Japanese tea leaves are used. Origin, harvest timing, and processing method are verified before production begins.

02

Cold Extraction

Leaves steep at low temperature for an extended period — 30× the duration of conventional brewing. This unlocks compounds that heat-based methods cannot capture.

03

Minimal Processing

No artificial flavoring, no added sugars, no shortcuts. The liquid is filtered and bottled with the minimum intervention necessary to preserve integrity.

04

Quality Verification

Each batch is verified for theanine concentration, color, aroma, and microbiological safety before release.
