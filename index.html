003

PROCESS

From your first inquiry to final delivery — a clear, guided journey.

01

Inquiry

Reach out via the inquiry form, email, or WhatsApp. Tell us about your product needs, intended use, and approximate volume. We respond within 1–2 business days.

02

Meeting & Consultation

We schedule a call or meeting to understand your brand, goals, and requirements in depth. Whether you need culinary-grade matcha for large-scale production or ceremonial-grade for a premium line, we listen before we recommend.

03

Proposal

Based on your required grade, volume, and budget, we present a tailored sourcing proposal — including sample options, pricing tiers, and supply timeline.

04

Agreement

Once terms are confirmed, we finalize the contract. We handle all necessary documentation and logistics coordination to ensure a smooth and transparent process.

05

Delivery

Your matcha is delivered on schedule, with full traceability from origin to destination. We remain your point of contact for reorders, quality checks, and ongoing supply needs.
