The Vision

Closer to the Source.

The next frontier for ARTERRA is direct farming partnerships — relationships built not at the trading desk, but in the field. We are developing direct ties with tea farmers in Japan and select international regions to give our clients unprecedented traceability and consistency.

This is not outsourcing. It is collaboration — working alongside the growers who shape the land, refining practices together, and creating a supply chain that neither side could build alone.

What This Means

What Direct Farming Unlocks

Full Traceability

Field-to-cup documentation. Know exactly where your matcha was grown, when it was harvested, and how it was processed.

Quality Co-Development

Work directly with farmers to refine shading duration, harvest timing, and processing for your specific application.

Supply Security

Dedicated allocations from partner farms — protecting your volume against market fluctuations and harvest variability.

Exclusive Access

Access to single-origin lots and experimental varieties not available through standard sourcing channels.
