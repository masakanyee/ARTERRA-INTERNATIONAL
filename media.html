Overview

#1 Most-Followed Matcha Media Globally

ARTERRA operates the world's most-followed matcha media platform. Through original content, brand storytelling, and ongoing collaborations, we connect the depth of Japanese tea culture with a global audience hungry for authenticity.

Our platform is not an advertising channel — it is a living editorial voice that brands trust to reach engaged, quality-conscious consumers around the world. Every piece of content we produce reflects the same standard we apply to our sourcing: no shortcuts, no noise.
